Our Story

Crafting Spaces
with Purpose

Shivam Decor is a trusted interior design studio based in Goregaon East, Mumbai. We bring a disciplined approach to design — one where aesthetics and functionality work together seamlessly to create spaces that are both beautiful and liveable.

From premium apartments in Lodha and Kalpataru to bungalows, villas, offices, and commercial establishments, our portfolio spans across Mumbai and beyond. We deliver turnkey solutions that remove every complexity from the client journey — handling design, carpentry, tiling, painting, and final styling under one roof.

How We Work

A Seamless
Design Process

01
Discovery & Brief

We listen. Your lifestyle, preferences, budget, and timeline form the foundation of every decision we make.

02
Concept & Design

Our designers develop mood boards, 3D layouts, material palettes, and detailed drawings for your approval.

03
Material Selection

We guide you through premium material choices — furniture, tiles, paint, laminates, stone, and hardware.

04
Execution

Our skilled craftsmen execute every element with precision — civil work, carpentry, tiling, painting, and wiring.

05
Handover & Styling

We deliver your completed space, styled and move-in ready, with a thorough walkthrough and quality check.
